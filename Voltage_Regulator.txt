*Input voltage sinusoidal with amplitude 12V and frequency of 50Hz

D1 a c diode
D2 b c diode
D3 0 a diode
D4 0 b diode
.model diode d
*Arranging diodes in full wave rectifier fashion

Cf c 0 0.2m

.model zener d BV 9
*Zener diode with break down voltage of 9V

RL 0 out 1k
*Load resistance of 1k ohm(within range 1k-2k)
